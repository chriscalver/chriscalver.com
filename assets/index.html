Klipboard Kitty

Klipboard Kitty is a windows application that allows you to share your clipboard data across all your Windows PC's. Maybe you'd like to see that image you copied to the word doc at the office? Anything you copy, cut or paste is saved to an SQL database for later access. My favorite feature is the "Clipboard AutoLoad". After one minute of inactivity the application automatically loads my email to the clipboard. I'm sure I've had to type my email into a form over a million times in my life. Never again. Klipboard Kitty runs quietly in the background and conveniently hides in the system tray. This is a .Net Windows Forms application written in C#. Download Ver 0.99
